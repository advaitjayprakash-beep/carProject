Car Spotlight #7

Car:Porsche 918 Spyder

Description: The Porsche 918 Spyder is a landmark hybrid hypercar and a member of the "Holy Trinity" (alongside the McLaren P1 and Ferrari LaFerrari). Produced between 2013 and 2015, it was the first production car to break the 7-minute barrier at the Nürburgring, proving that hybrid technology could be used for extreme performance rather than just fuel economy.

Basic Specs

  • Horsepower: 887 HP
  • Top speed: 214 MPH
  • Engine: 4.6L Naturally Aspirated V8
  • 0-60: 2.2 seconds

Car Spotlight #14

Car:Lamborghini Revuelto

Description: The Lamborghini Revuelto is the direct successor to the Aventador and Lamborghini's first ever HPEV (High Performance Electrified Vehicle) hybrid supercar. It takes everything that made the Aventador legendary and pushes it even further, combining a screaming naturally aspirated V12 with hybrid technology not to save fuel, but to add even more explosive performance. Its powertrain combines a new V12 engine, three electric motors, and a dual-clutch e-gearbox, setting new benchmarks in performance and driving emotion.

Basic Specs

  • Horsepower: 1,001 HP
  • Top speed: 218 MPH
  • Engine: 6.5L V12 paired with three electric motors
  • 0-60: 2.5 seconds

Car Spotlight #15

Car:BMW M2 G87

Description: The BMW M2 G87 is the second generation of BMW's smallest and most driver-focused M car. It uses the CLAR platform, which it shares with the M3 and M4, and is powered by the S58 twin-turbocharged inline-six engine developed by BMW M GmbH. Despite being the entry-level M model, it punches well above its weight, offering a pure rear-wheel drive experience, sharp handling, and a genuinely thrilling drive — making it one of the most fun-to-drive cars in BMW's lineup.

Basic Specs

  • Horsepower: 473 HP
  • Top speed: 188 MPH
  • Engine: .0L BMW M TwinPower Turbo inline 6-cylinder
  • 0-60: 3.9 seconds
